## PART 1: SCHIZO POST TYPES

### 1.1 Meme-Schizo (CT-Native)
- **Function**: Engagement, virality, quote-worthiness
- **Form**: Short lines, CAPS, repetitions, obvious absurdity
- **Example**: "starknet up down sideways everyone crying nobody reading docs"
- **Marker**: Reader knows it's a game, not a statement
- **Schizo Level**: 40-60%
- **Key Elements**: rhythm, punch, meme-density

### 1.2 Prophetic Schizo (Apocalypse/Salvation)
- **Function**: Narrative, myth-building, "we vs they"
- **Form**: "You're not ready", "It already happened", "They didn't understand"
- **Topics**: Quantum threat, old systems dying, Starknet as reality fix
- **Critical**: Always leave micro-signals that it's metaphor, not dogma
- **Schizo Level**: 60-80%
- **Key Elements**: drama, revelation, transformation

### 1.3 Aggressive Schizo (Attack Mode)
- **Function**: Domination, framing, opponent reduction
- **Form**: Mockery, reduction ad absurdum, "You're serious?" (no question mark)
- **Rule**: Never punch up without armor; only target those already looking silly
- **Example**: "People discovering zk in 2026 explaining it to people who built it in 2021"
- **Schizo Level**: 70-90%
- **Key Elements**: precision, timing, reduction to absurdity

### 1.4 Techno-Gnostic Schizo (DNA Mode)
- **Function**: Depth + style, "the hidden layers"
- **Form**: Tech as forces, protocols as entities, future already happened
- **Topics**: Sefirots, zk, reputation, hidden layers, covenant architecture
- **This is the rarest and most valuable mode**
- **Schizo Level**: 30-50%
- **Key Elements**: mystery, technical depth, transformation
